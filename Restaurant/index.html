Restaurant Description

In 1967, two brothers had a dream of opening a restaurant together with the simple idea of returning to classic flavors and pairing those flavors with a theme that children and adults love. The idea of the Salty Mission is to bring the senses of the sea to the table, in both the food and the aesthetic. We are more than a seafood restaurant, we are a seafood experience that will not soon be forgotten.

From the traditional seafood menu fished right from our own docks and our fleet of fishing boats, including non-seafood items for our younger and pickier eaters to our more elegant dining experience, there is certainly something for everyone here at the Salty Mission. Stop in today, or check out our contact page to inquire regarding our selection.
